# Prevention of Sexual Harassment (PoSH)

## 1. What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

Sexual harassment includes any **unwelcome behaviour of a sexual nature** that creates an intimidating, hostile, offensive, or uncomfortable environment.

### A. Physical Behaviour
- Unwanted touching, hugging, patting, or brushing against someone
- Attempting to kiss or make physical advances
- Blocking someone’s way or invading personal space
- Physical assault

### B. Verbal Behaviour
- Sexual comments about appearance, body, or clothing
- Sexually coloured remarks or jokes
- Repeated requests for dates despite refusal
- Asking for sexual favours in exchange for promotions, benefits, or opportunities

### C. Non-Verbal / Online Behaviour
- Staring, leering, or suggestive gestures
- Sharing inappropriate images, videos, or messages
- Sending sexual texts, emails, or emojis
- Displaying offensive or explicit material in the workplace

> Any behaviour that is **unwelcome and sexual in nature**, whether physical, verbal, or non-verbal, can amount to sexual harassment.

---

## 2. What would you do if you face or witness such behaviour?

### If I Face Sexual Harassment:
1. Clearly tell the person to stop (if it is safe to do so).
2. Document the incident (date, time, location, witnesses, screenshots if any).
3. Report the incident to the Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) or HR as per the PoSH policy.
4. Seek support from a trusted colleague, senior, or counsellor.
5. Cooperate with the investigation process.

### If I Witness Sexual Harassment:
1. Support the affected person and listen without judgment.
2. Encourage them to report the incident.
3. Offer to be a witness if required.
4. Report the behaviour to HR/ICC if necessary.
5. Do not ignore, encourage, or participate in inappropriate behaviour.

---

## Key Principles
- Sexual harassment is about the **impact on the victim**, not the intention of the person.
- Even a single serious incident can qualify as harassment.
- Every employee has the responsibility to maintain a safe and respectful workplace.
