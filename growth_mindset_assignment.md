# Grit and Growth Mindset Assignment

## Question 1 – Grit
Grit means working continuously toward long-term goals with passion and patience. Success comes from consistent effort and not giving up even after failures.

## Question 2 – Growth Mindset Introduction
Growth mindset is the belief that intelligence and skills can improve with practice, learning, and effort instead of thinking abilities are fixed.

## Question 3 – Internal Locus of Control
Internal locus of control means believing that our success and failure mainly depend on our own actions and decisions. The key idea is that when we focus on what we can control, we stay motivated and work harder to achieve our goals.

## Question 4 – Key points to build Growth Mindset
- Believe that skills can improve with practice
- Accept challenges and learn from them
- Learn from mistakes instead of fearing them
- Focus on effort and improvement
- Take feedback positively
- Stay consistent and patient

## Question 5 – My action plan to build Growth Mindset
- Practice coding and testing concepts daily
- Learn from mistakes during practice and interviews
- Set small weekly learning goals
- Ask feedback from mentors and improve
- Stay consistent and track my progress
